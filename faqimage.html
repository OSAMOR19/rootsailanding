FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Got questions? We've got answers. Everything you need to know about bringing authentic Afrobeat rhythms to your productions.

What is ROOTS

ROOTS is an intelligent AI-powered drum plugin that listens to your tracks, understands your intent, and helps you find or generate authentic African drum loops that fit your music instantly. It combines a rich curated drum library, AI-powered recommendations, and full creative control — all in one plugin.

Does ROOTS work in a DAW or as a standalone plugin?

ROOTS runs as a standalone application and it allows you to drag, drop, and play your drums without limits.

How does ROOTS use AI?

ROOTS uses AI to analyze your track’s rhythm, groove, and style. It recommends drum loops from the library that fit your music. You stay in control — ROOTS only suggests, you decide what to use.

What is included in ROOTS?

ROOTS includes: ● A massive library of authentic African drum loops ● AI-powered drum recommendations ● Ability to analyze your audio or DAW track ● Customizable prompts for groove creation ● Royalty-free, credit-free usage

Does ROOTS require an internet connection to work?

Yes, ROOTS requires an internet connection to seamlessly load sounds from the cloud. Once loaded, all sounds work offline within your sessions.

Are your samples royalty-free?

Yes! Every drum loop in ROOTS is royalty-free. You can use them in your tracks, beats, and productions without restrictions.

How do I manage my plan?

Users can manage their account, subscription, and downloads through the dedicated dashboard on the ROOTS landing page.

Can I upload my own samples?

Yes! ROOTS can analyze and work with your own audio files, allowing you to integrate custom samples and loops into its AI-assisted workflow.

How often is the drum library updated?

The ROOTS library is updated regularly with new, authentic African drum loops, ensuring you always have fresh sounds to create with.

Does ROOTS work on Mac and Windows?

Yes, ROOTS is fully compatible with both Mac and Windows operating systems.

Is ROOTS beginner-friendly?

Absolutely! ROOTS is designed to be intuitive and accessible for beginners, while still offering advanced features and creative control for professional producers.
